Introduction:

Education is a fundamental human right that enables individuals to lead dignified lives. However, access to education is often limited due to various social, economic, and geographical factors. One of the primary obstacles to education is the lack of access to educational resources such as books. This issue is particularly prevalent in underprivileged communities, where students cannot afford to buy books or have limited access to libraries. This project aims to address this issue by providing access to books through a book bank. Goals and Objectives: The primary goal of the project is to promote education among underprivileged students by providing them with access to books and ensure that all students have access to education, regardless of their financial background. The following are the specific objectives of the project: Collecting books from donors and building a book bank. Distributing books to underprivileged students in the community. Providing financial assistance to needy students for their education. Creating awareness about the importance of education among the economically weaker sections of society. Helping students develop essential life skills that will help them succeed in their careers. Improving academic performance amongst underprivileged students. Providing career guidance and counselling to students to help them make informed career choices.
