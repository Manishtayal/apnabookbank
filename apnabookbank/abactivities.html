Activities:

To achieve the above objectives, the PPYMis engaged in the following activities: Identifying needy students: PPYMidentifies students who require financial assistance to continue their education. These students are selected based on their academic performance, financial background, and their willingness to learn. Providing financial assistance: PPYM provides financial assistance to selected students to cover their education-related expenses such as tuition fees, books and stationery. Career guidance and counselling: PPYM provides career guidance and counselling to students to help them make informed career choices. This includes, inviting experts to speak to students, and providing information on various career options. Providing life skills training: PPYMis planning to conduct various workshops and training sessions to help students develop essential life skills such as time management, communication skills and teamwork.

Strategies:

The project will adopt the following strategies to achieve its objectives: Collection of books: PPYM will collect old and new books from donors, including individuals, schools and organizations. Sorting and categorisation of books: The books collected will be sorted and categorised based on age group and academic level. Establishment of a book bank : The PanchParmesthiYuvaMnadalhas established an‘Apna Book Bank’ where students can borrow books for a specific period free of cost with a nominal security charge which is completely refundable after returning books. Distribution of books: The books will be distributed to underprivileged students in the community through various channels mainly through ‘Apna Book Bank’ premises, including camps and community centres. Encouraging reading: PPYM will organise reading events and workshops to encourage students to read and develop a love for learning.

Outcomes:

The project aims to achieve the following outcomes: Increased access to educational resources: The book bank will provide underprivileged students with access to books they would not otherwise have access to. Improved academic performance: Access to educational resources will help students improve their academic performance and increase their chances of pursuing higher education. Promotion of literacy: Encouraging reading amongst students will promote literacy and help develop critical thinking skills. Community engagement: The project will engage the community by providing a platform for individuals and organizations to donate books and contribute to the education of underprivileged students.

Impact:

The project has the potential to have a significant impact on the community by promoting education among underprivileged students. The project will not only provide access to educational resources but also promote a culture of learning and development. Since its inception, PPYM has helped numerous students complete their education. The impact of PPYM can be seen in the following ways: Increased access to education: PPYM has helped increase access to education for students from economically weaker sections of society by providing books. Improved academic performance: The financial assistance provided by the PPYM has helped students focus on their studies and improve their academic performance. Enhanced life skills: The life skills training provided by the PPYM has helped students develop essential life skills that will help them succeed in their careers. Informed career choices: The career guidance and counselling provided by PPYM have helped students make informed career choices and pursue their dreams. Increased motivation to learn: Having access to books and learning materials can increase a student's motivation to learn and succeed in school. This can have a positive impact on their academic performance and future prospects. Positive impact on mental health: Access to books and learning materials can also have a positive impact on a student's mental health, providing them with a sense of purpose and belonging The project has achieved the following outcomes:
